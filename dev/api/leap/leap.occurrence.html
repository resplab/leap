    equation(sex: Sex, age: int, year: int, has_family_history: bool, dose: int) → float[source]¶

    Compute the asthma incidence / prevalence for a given risk factor combination.

    \[\zeta_{\lambda} = \sigma(\beta_{\eta} + \log(\omega_{\lambda}) - \alpha)\]

    where:

    • \(\beta_{\eta} = \sigma^{-1}(\eta(t))\) is determined by the output of Model 1

    • \(\omega_{\lambda}\) is the odds ratio for asthma incidence / prevalence based on family history and antibiotic doses

    • \(\alpha\) is the incidence / prevalence correction term

    Parameters:¶
    sex: Sex¶

    The sex of the agent.

    age: int¶

    The age of the agent.

    year: int¶

    The calendar year.

    has_family_history: bool¶

    True if one or more parents of the agent has asthma, otherwise False.

    dose: int¶

    The number of courses of antibiotics taken during the first year of life.

    calculate_odds_ratio_fam_history(age: int, fam_hist: int) → float[source]¶

    Calculate the odds ratio for asthma prevalence based on family history.

    \[\log(\omega(f_{\lambda})) = \beta_{\text{fhx_0}} \cdot f + \beta_{\text{fhx_age}} \cdot (\text{min}(a, 5) - 3) \cdot f\]

    where:

    • \(\beta_{\text{fhx_xxx}}\) is a constant coefficient

    • \(a\) is the age

    • \(f\) is the family history of asthma; 1 = at least one parent has asthma, 0 = neither parent has asthma

    Parameters:¶
    age: int¶

    The age of the individual in years.

    fam_hist: int¶

    The family history of asthma, an integer in [0, 1], where 1 indicates at least one parent has asthma.

    Returns:¶

    The odds ratio for asthma prevalence based on family history and age.

    calculate_odds_ratio_abx(age: int, dose: int) → float[source]¶

    Calculate the odds ratio for asthma prevalence based on antibiotic exposure.

    \[\begin{split}\log(\omega(d_{\lambda})) = \begin{cases} \beta_{\text{abx_0}} + \beta_{\text{abx_age}} \cdot \text{min}(a, 7) + \beta_{\text{abx_dose}} \cdot \text{min}(d, 3) && d > 0 \text{ and } a \leq 7 \\ \\ 0 && \text{otherwise} \end{cases}\end{split}\]

    where:

    • \(\beta_{\text{abx_xxx}}\) is a constant coefficient

    • \(a\) is the age

    • \(d\) is the number of courses of antibiotics taken during the first year of life

    Parameters:¶
    age: int¶

    The age of the individual in years.

    dose: int¶

    The number of antibiotic courses taken in the first year of life, an integer in [0, 5], where 5 indicates 5 or more courses.

    Returns:¶

    The odds ratio for asthma prevalence based on antibiotic exposure and age.

    property parameters : dict¶

    A dictionary containing the following keys:

    • β0 (float): A constant parameter.

    • βsex (float): The parameter for the sex term, i.e. \(\beta_{\text{sex}} * \text{sex}\).

    • βyear (float): The parameter for the year term, i.e. \(\beta_{\text{year}} * \text{year}\).

    • βsexyear (float): The parameter to be multiplied by sex and year, i.e. \(\beta_{\text{sexyear}} * \text{year} * \text{sex}\).

    • βage (list[float]): An array of 5 parameters to be multiplied by powers of age, i.e.

      \[\beta_{\text{age}1} * \text{age} + \beta_{\text{age}2} * \text{age}^2 + \beta_{\text{age}3} * \text{age}^3 + \beta_{\text{age}4} * \text{age}^4 + \beta_{\text{age}5} * \text{age}^5\]

    • βsexage (list[float]): An array of 5 parameters to be multiplied by the sex and powers of age, i.e.

      \[\begin{split}\beta_{\text{sexage}1} * \text{sex} * \text{age} + \beta_{\text{sexage}2} * \text{sex} * \text{age}^2 + \beta_{\text{sexage}3} * \text{sex} * \text{age}^3 + \\ \beta_{\text{sexage}4} * \text{sex} * \text{age}^4 + \beta_{\text{sexage}5} * \text{sex} * \text{age}^5\end{split}\]

    • β_fam_hist (dict): A dictionary of 2 parameters to be multiplied by functions of age. See calculate_odds_ratio_fam_history.

    • β_abx (dict): A dictionary of 3 parameters to be multiplied by functions of age and antibiotic dose. See calculate_odds_ratio_abx.

    property poly_parameters : dict[str, list[float]]¶

    A dictionary containing the following keys:

    • alpha_age (list[float]): The alpha vector from the normalization of the training data in the poly function. Length = degree of age polynomial = 5.

    • norm2_age (list[float]): The \(\text{norm}^2\) vector from the normalization of the training data in the poly function. Length = degree of age polynomial + 1 = 6.

    crude_occurrence(sex: Sex, age: int, year: int) → float[source]¶

    Calculate the crude asthma incidence.

    \[\eta^{(i)} = \sum_{m=0}^1 \beta_{01m} t^{(i)} \cdot (s^{(i)})^m + \sum_{k=0}^{5} \sum_{m=0}^{1} \beta_{k0m} \cdot (a^{(i)})^k \cdot (s^{(i)})^m\]

    where:

    • \(\eta^{(i)}\) is the crude asthma incidence

    • \(\beta_{k\ell m}\) is the coefficient for the feature \((a^{(i)})^k \cdot (t^{(i)})^{\ell} \cdot (s^{(i)})^m\)

    • \(a^{(i)}\) is the age

    • \(t^{(i)}\) is the year

    • \(s^{(i)}\) is the sex

    Parameters:¶
    sex: Sex¶

    The sex of the agent.

    age: int¶

    The age of the agent.

    year: int¶

    The calendar year.

    Returns:¶

    A float representing the crude asthma incidence for the given year, age, and sex.

    • βsexyear (list[float]): An array of 2 parameters to be multiplied by sex and powers of year, i.e.

      \[\beta_{\text{sexyear}1} * \text{sex} * \text{year} + \beta_{\text{sexyear}2} * \text{sex} * \text{year}^2\]

    • βage (list[float]): An array of 5 parameters to be multiplied by powers of age, i.e.

      \[\beta_{\text{age}1} * \text{age} + \beta_{\text{age}2} * \text{age}^2 + \beta_{\text{age}3} * \text{age}^3 + \beta_{\text{age}4} * \text{age}^4 + \beta_{\text{age}5} * \text{age}^5\]

    • βsexage (list[float]): An array of 5 parameters to be multiplied by the sex and powers of age, i.e.

      \[\begin{split}\beta_{\text{sexage}1} * \text{sex} * \text{age} + \beta_{\text{sexage}2} * \text{sex} * \text{age}^2 + \beta_{\text{sexage}3} * \text{sex} * \text{age}^3 + \\ \beta_{\text{sexage}4} * \text{sex} * \text{age}^4 + \beta_{\text{sexage}5} * \text{sex} * \text{age}^5\end{split}\]

    • βyearage (list[float]): An array of 10 parameters to be multiplied by powers of age and year, i.e.

      \[\begin{split}\beta_{\text{yearage}1} * \text{year} * \text{age} + \beta_{\text{yearage}2} * \text{year}^2 * \text{age} + \\ \beta_{\text{yearage}3} * \text{year} * \text{age}^2 + \beta_{\text{yearage}4} * \text{year}^2 * \text{age}^2 + \\ \beta_{\text{yearage}5} * \text{year} * \text{age}^3 + \beta_{\text{yearage}6} * \text{year}^2 * \text{age}^3 + \\ \beta_{\text{yearage}7} * \text{year} * \text{age}^4 + \beta_{\text{yearage}8} * \text{year}^2 * \text{age}^4 + \\ \beta_{\text{yearage}9} * \text{year} * \text{age}^5 + \beta_{\text{yearage}10} * \text{year}^2 * \text{age}^5\end{split}\]

      \[\begin{split}\beta_{\text{sexyearage}1} * \text{sex} * \text{year} * \text{age} + \beta_{\text{sexyearage}2} * \text{sex} * \text{year}^2 * \text{age} + \\ \beta_{\text{sexyearage}3} * \text{sex} * \text{year} * \text{age}^2 + \beta_{\text{sexyearage}4} * \text{sex} * \text{year}^2 * \text{age}^2 + \\ \beta_{\text{sexyearage}5} * \text{sex} * \text{year} * \text{age}^3 + \beta_{\text{sexyearage}6} * \text{sex} * \text{year}^2 * \text{age}^3 + \\ \beta_{\text{sexyearage}7} * \text{sex} * \text{year} * \text{age}^4 + \beta_{\text{sexyearage}8} * \text{sex} * \text{year}^2 * \text{age}^4 + \\ \beta_{\text{sexyearage}9} * \text{sex} * \text{year} * \text{age}^5 + \beta_{\text{sexyearage}10} * \text{sex} * \text{year}^2 * \text{age}^5\end{split}\]

    • β_abx (dict): A dictionary of 3 parameters to be multiplied by functions of age and antibiotic dose. See calculate_odds_ratio_abx.

    crude_occurrence(sex: Sex, age: int, year: int) → float[source]¶

    Calculate the crude asthma prevalence.

    \[\eta^{(i)} = \sum_{k=0}^{5} \sum_{\ell=0}^2 \sum_{m=0}^1 \beta_{k \ell m} \cdot (a^{(i)})^k \cdot (t^{(i)})^{\ell} \cdot (s^{(i)})^m\]

    where:

    • \(\beta_{k\ell m}\) is the coefficient for the feature \((a^{(i)})^k \cdot (t^{(i)})^{\ell} \cdot (s^{(i)})^m\)

    • \(a^{(i)}\) is the age

    • \(t^{(i)}\) is the year

    • \(s^{(i)}\) is the sex

    There are \(6 * 3 * 2 = 36\) coefficients in the prevalence model.

    Parameters:¶
    sex: Sex¶

    The sex of the agent.

    age: int¶

    The age of the agent.

    year: int¶

    The calendar year.

    Returns:¶

    A float representing the crude asthma prevalence for the given year, age, and sex.
